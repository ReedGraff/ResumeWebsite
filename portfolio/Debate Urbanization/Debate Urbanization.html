Hey, this is some debate research me and my partner did over two and a half weeks for a debate tournament (state tournament), debating the resolution "Resolved on balance, the benefits of urbanization in West Africa outweigh the harms.". The research, speeches, notes, and rebuttals total around 60 pages (might be wrong... That count is added with a few files that I didn't include). I was primarily responsible for the Pro Case, and my partner was primarily responsible for the Con Case.

DISCLAIMER The following research is only intended to be used for educational purposes. The following files shall not be used against me, as the files are only a testament to my dedication and are not effected by personal opinion (nor do they reflect my personal opinion), as the point of debate is to argue for both sides, regardless of personal stance
