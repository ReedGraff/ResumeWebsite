IcoFoam primarily uses the PISO algorithm (Pressure-Implicit with Splitting of Operators) to solve the continuity equation... The IcoFoam also uses the momentum equation.

IcoFoam

PISO Equation: ∇∙u=0

Momentum Equation: (∂/∂t)∙(u)+∇∙(u⊗u)−∇∙(ν∇u)=−∇p
