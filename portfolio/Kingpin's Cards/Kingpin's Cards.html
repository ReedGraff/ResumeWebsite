More Info

The basic idea is that I make NFC tag business cards to replace business cards as a whole... It is currently incredibly inneficient to purchase 500+ business cards when you can easily make one reusable business card... I made an NFC/RFID tag business card that works, I currently have mine in a 3d printed exoskeleton, however I am working on laser cutting an either metal or delrin skin for the tag.

I have put in a lot of work inot this project, however I do not have enough info that I am willing to release yet...
